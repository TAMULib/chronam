Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

TAMU Updates

The Texas A&M University Newspaper Collection consists of digitized newspapers that have been or currently are produced by Texas A&M University. These newspapers were digitized from original copies held at Cushing Memorial Library and Archives and microfilmed copies from Sterling C. Evans Library.

Why are there missing issues?
Unfortunately the University Libraries do not have complete holdings of all the newspapers produced at Texas A&M University and as a result there are missing issues.

I have a copy/copies of missing issues, are you interested in them?
Yes we are seeking any missing issues so that we can complete our holdings. Please visit the Contribute page on how you can help accomplish that.

I have discovered an error, who should I contact?
If you happen to find an error within the collection (missing pages, pages out of order, broken links) please contact Cushing Memorial Library and Archives at cushing-library@tamu.edu or 979-845-1951 so that we can address the issues as soon as possible.

Will other newspapers held within the Libraries be digitized?
At this time this site is only focused on newspapers that have been produced by Texas A&M University.
